Sorrowsworn of the Hungry
Medium, monstrosity, Neutral Evil
AC 17    Initiative +4 (14)
HP 225 (30d8 + 90)
Speed walk 30
Mod Save
STR 19 +4 +4
DEX 10 +0 +0
CON 17 +3 +3
Mod Save
INT 6 -2 -2
WIS 11 +0 +0
CHA 6 -2 -2
Resistances while in dim light or darkness
Senses darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 10
Languages Common
CR 11
Traits

Life Hunger. If a creature within 60 feet of the sorrowsworn regains hit points, the sorrowsworn gains two benefits until the end of its next turn: it has advantage on attack rolls, and its Bite deals an extra 22 (4d10) necrotic damage on a hit.

Actions

Multiattack. The sorrowsworn makes one Bite attack and one Claw attack.

Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage plus 13 (3d8) necrotic damage.

Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. 18 (4d6 + 4) slashing damage. If the target is Medium or smaller, it is grappled (escape DC 16), and it is restrained until the grapple ends. While grappling a creature, the sorrowsworn can't make a Claw attack.
