Kobold Tinkerer
Small, humanoid, lawful evil
AC 12    Initiative +4 (14)
HP 5 (3d6)
Speed 30 ft, hover 4
Mod Save
STR 7 -2 -2
DEX 14 +2 +2
CON 10 +0 +0
Mod Save
INT 15 +2 +2
WIS 7 -2 -2
CHA 9 -1 -1
Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
Languages Common, Draconic
CR 1/8
Traits

Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, the kobold has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

Pack Tactics. The kobold has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the kobold's allies is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

Gear (Air Boots). The kobold has a set of magical books that allow it to hover as a bonus action.

Actions

Dagger. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.

Alchemical Flame. The kobold unleashes fire in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) fire damage on a failed saving throw, or half as much damage on a successful one.
