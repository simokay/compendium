Traits

Iron Mind. Iridescent nagas are immune to all mind-influencing effects.

Conflict Averse. Iridescent nagas avoid combat if possible, preferring to hypnotize their foes and suggest other courses of action. Should hostilities ensue, iridescent nagas prefer the high ground, where they can employ their spells and hypnotic swaying while staying out of their foes' reach.

Actions

Hypnotic Sway. By swaying in place, an iridescent naga can produce an effect like that of the hypnotism spell that affects every creature within 30 feet. The save DC 18 Charisma to resist.

Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature in the snake's space. Hit: 15 (2d8+8) piercing damage plus 5 (1d6) poison damage.

Spellcasting. The naga is a 5th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks). The snake has following spells prepared:

  • At Will. Poison Spray, Message, Shape Water
  • 3/Day Each. Disguise Self, Sanctuary, Sleep
  • 2/Day Each. See Invisibility, Invisibility, Enthrall
  • 1/Day Each. Remove Curse, Wall of Water
