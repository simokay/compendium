Giant Shadow Ooze
Huge, ooze, unaligned

HP 85 (16d10 + 30)
Speed 20 ft., climb 20 ft.
Mod Save
STR 16 +3 +3
DEX 5 -3 -3

Mod Save
INT 1 -5 -5
WIS 6 -2 -2
CHA 1 -5 -5
Damage Immunities acid, cold, lightning, slashing
Senses blindsight 60 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 8
Languages
CR 4
Traits

Amorphous. The pudding can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

Shadow Form. A creature that touches the pudding or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 4 (2d8) necrotic damage.

Ooze Cover. The pudding can fling part of itself onto surfaces, especially those that are a source of light

Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, the kobold has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

Actions

Pseudopod. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage plus 18 (4d8) necrotic damage.
