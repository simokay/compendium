Traits

Air Form. The elemental can enter and end its turn in other creatures’ spaces and pass through an opening as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

Elemental Nature. An elemental doesn’t require air, sustenance, or sleep.

Actions

Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 24 (6d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage.

Whirlwind (Recharge 2–3). The elemental takes the form of a whirlwind. When a creature shares its space with the whirlwind for the first time during this movement, that creature makes a DC 15 Strength saving throw . On a failure, the creature is carried inside the elemental’s space until the whirlwind ends, taking 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage for each 10 feet it is carried, and falls prone at the end of the movement. The whirlwind can carry one Large creature or up to four Medium or smaller creatures.

Summon Dust Mephit. As an action the air elemental can summon a Dust Mephit
