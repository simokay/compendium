Traits

Keen Hearing and Smell. The creature has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

Undead Fortitude. If damage reduces the snake to 0 hit points, it must make a Constitution saving throw with a DC of 5 + the damage taken, unless the damage is radiant or from a critical hit. On a success, the snake drops to 1 hit point instead.

Actions

Multiattack. The creature makes two attacks: one with its bite and one or more with its claws.

Death Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 5) piercing damage. If the target is a humanoid, it must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or take an addition 2d8 necrotic damage.

Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 7 (2d6 + 6) slashing damage.
