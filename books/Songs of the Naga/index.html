Songs of the Naga

A small notebook of maybe 10 songs about Naga such as:

Down in the temples where the black roots breathe,
Ss'keth ss ssethressh massis ss’thra’kha briss, I heard the hiss of an eternal seethe. Ish sssha’hir mis sssith ss asi s’zeer’nithe sess. Eyes like moons in a deathless void,
Saa'keth eyesh liss ssha’muun ss dra'vhoid, Scales that shimmer where time's destroyed. Ssska’aleth wis shiverrr wis tis’is dessek.
